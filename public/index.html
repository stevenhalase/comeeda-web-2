About COmeeda

What is COmeeda?

COmeeda is a tax-exempt non-profit food distribution service that systematically recycles what would ordinarily be wasted food, and delivers the excess food to those in need throughout Colorado; launching first in Denver.
Our partners will have the opportunity to use our app to connect with hundreds of local volunteers that are available to pick-up food and deliver it to nearby shelters, food banks, veteran shelters and those families in need. If you're interested in helping with our efforts to fight hunger, then please consider joining our movement and become a Volunteer.

We are currently looking to build a strong volunteer base for when COmeeda goes live. If you have any interest in becoming a volunteer, please email our Executive Director, Omar Castro, at ocastro@comeeda.org, or our Director of Operations, Brian Dumoulin, at bdumoulin@comeeda.org with your contact information and they'll get the process going. Also, please pass your enthusiasm on to friends and family and encourage them to jump on board and help us bring change and the future of food donation.

Once COmeeda is ready to go live, and you've passed our detailed background check(s), one of our coordinators will reach out and provide your login information so you can start making your first deliveries!

Through our app, we'll provide you with your own Dashboard where you can track the amount of pickups, miles driven, and hours volunteered, for your convenience. We do our best to take care of our volunteers and based off the amount of pickups and hours volunteered, you'll be added to our "All-star" team where you'll receive social media recognition and exclusive gifts that have been donated to COmeeda. If you are the competetive type, you will even be able to see how you stack up against other COmeeda volunteers.
