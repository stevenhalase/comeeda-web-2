About COmeeda

What is COmeeda?

COmeeda is a tax-exempt non-profit food distribution service that systematically recycles what would ordinarily be wasted food, and delivers the excess food to those in need throughout Colorado; launching first in Denver.
Our partners will have the opportunity to use our app to connect with hundreds of local volunteers that are available to pick-up food and deliver it to nearby shelters, food banks, veteran shelters and those families in need. If you're interested in helping with our efforts to fight hunger, then please consider joining our movement and become a Volunteer.

We are currently looking to build a strong volunteer base for when COmeeda goes live. If you have any interest in becoming a volunteer, please email our Executive Director, Omar Castro, at ocastro@comeeda.org, or our Director of Operations, Brian Dumoulin, at bdumoulin@comeeda.org with your contact information and they'll get the process going. Features

Great features you'll love.

Version 1.0 of COmeeda will push the boundaries with great in app features focusing on ease of use for our users that includes great design. You can also count on the COmeeda team to continuously add new cutting edge features that will make using the app fun and easy. Any features you think would add to your COmeeda experience are welcomed, so feel free to email someone on the team with your ideas.

Partner Mode

Our business partners can log in to schedule weekly, daily, or monthly pickups by adding them to our driver’s schedules in real time so they know that we can be there when they need us.

Donate Mode

Anyone can log in as a Donor to request a pickup from one of our many amazing volunteers. Once you request a pickup, the nearest logged in volunteer will be given step by step directions to you so your donation can get to one of our partner facilities in a timely fashion.

Data Tracking

After every pickup, your volunteer stats will update in your dashboard. This makes it easy for our volunteers to be able to obtain the tax breaks they are due for all their hard work and kindness.

Monthly Contests

Our volunteers will be stacked up against each other for some friendly competition where our top volunteers will earn special prizes each month for their generosity. It's one way for us at COmeeda to say thank you for being so great!

In App Messaging

We appreciate our volunteers, donors, and partners for doing what they do, but we also care about their privacy. With COmeeda, you can communicate directly between each other within the in app messaging system so you do not have to give out your personal information.

Free of Charge

Downloading COmeeda is 100% free! While we do accept donations to help build and grow COmeeda, we do not feel anyone should have to spend money to do good for their community. Once COmeeda goes live, you will be able to download it in the app store, so keep your eyes open and pass along the good vibes!

Mission

Mission Statement.

COmeeda’s goal is to alleviate hunger in the Denver Metro Area by soliciting, collecting, growing and packaging food in conjunction with distributing it through a network of service agencies and partners that serve our target group. COmeeda is an application that works in conjunction with similar nonprofit organizations in Denver and surrounding Counties, to aggregate our volunteers in one network. Our partners will have the opportunity to use one application to connect with hundreds of local volunteers that are currently available to pick-up food and deliver it to the nearest shelter or food bank. COmeeda receives support from the county, charitable organizations, grants, and corporate sponsorships. COmeeda’s co-founder’s include: Omar Castro (Executive Director), Brian Dumoulin (Director of Operations), and Steven Halase (Director of Engineering).

Our client base include low-income individuals and families who need emergency assistance to feed their families. It is estimated that nearly half of those that can benefit from COmeeda are children and young adults under the age of 18 years. COmeeda actively solicits food from local growers, retailers, wholesalers, restaurants, caterers, technology companies, and grocery stores for immediate distribution to local nonprofits like COmeeda, homeless shelters and food banks.
